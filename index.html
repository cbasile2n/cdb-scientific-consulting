About

Scientific direction

His work centers on integrating mechanistic models with data-driven methods to understand complex biological systems, particularly in drug response, immuno-oncology, and broader biomedical applications. More recently, he has explored how AI can be used to build more interpretable and biophysically grounded tools for healthcare and disease modeling.

Background and perspective

His work is shaped by a dual influence: engineering rigor and medical purpose. His father, Juan Carlos Basile, was a civil engineer who contributed to major infrastructure projects in the United States and later applied engineering principles to public service in Argentina. His mother, Dr. Liliana Luz Carnival, was an infectious disease specialist whose clinical dedication left a lasting mark on how he thinks about science, medicine, and real-world relevance.

Core Service
