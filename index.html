Modeling Biology. Advancing Medicine.

A computational approach to understanding complex biological systems through the integration of machine learning, mechanistic modeling, and real-world biomedical data.

About

CDB Scientific focuses on building computational frameworks that bridge data-driven methods with mechanistic understanding of biological systems.

The goal is not only to generate predictions, but to uncover structure within complex systems — enabling more interpretable, transferable, and biologically grounded insights.

Approach

Traditional machine learning approaches often treat biological systems as black boxes. In contrast, this work integrates:

• Mechanistic modeling (QSP, dynamical systems) • Statistical learning and AI • Multi-modal biological data integration

This combined perspective allows for exploration of system behavior, identification of key variables, and generation of hypotheses that extend beyond purely statistical correlations.

Areas of Focus

• Drug response modeling and simulation • Biological system dynamics and disease progression • Integration of molecular, clinical, and population-level data • Interpretable AI for biomedical applications

Philosophy

Biological systems are inherently complex, but not arbitrary. By combining mathematical structure with data-driven approaches, it is possible to move toward models that are both predictive and explanatory.

The long-term objective is to contribute to a shift from reactive medicine toward more predictive, system-level understanding of disease.
